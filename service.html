What's Included:

I offer overnight stays in your home with your pet(s). I charge $35 per dog per night and $25 per cat per night. Any additional caged/tanked pets are free of charge. I follow whatever your pets' normal routines are.

  • Feeding and Water
  • Waste Scoops
  • Administer medications if applicable
  • Walks/Play
  • Check Mail/Water Plants/Trash
  • Regular photo/video updates
