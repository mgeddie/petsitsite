About Me

Hello and welcome!

I give traveling pet owners in the San Antonio area peace of mind by staying overnight in your home in order to stick to your pets' normal routine. My name is Meg and I've been petsitting in San Antonio for around 11 years. I have 7 years working in an animal hopsital kennel. I grew up with my first dog, Brownie and now I have 2 cats. I follow all your pets' dietary, exercise and medical routines. I'm available for overnight stays in your home and I'm never gone for more than a typical work day. I regularly check in with texts and photos so you can see how your pet is doing. My goal is to give your pets the same loving treatment as you do.
